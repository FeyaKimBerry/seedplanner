Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Seedplanner ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Seedplanner (the "Service"), available at https://seedplanner.app. By using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Google account profile. When you sign in with Google, we receive your basic profile information (name, email address, and profile picture) to identify your account and personalize the app.
  • Your financial planning data. The income, expenses, goals, debts, assets and settings you enter into Seedplanner.
  • Local technical data. Information stored in your browser's local storage to keep the app working between sessions.

We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the Service — saving, displaying and projecting your financial plan.
  • To authenticate you and sync your data to your own Google Drive (see below).
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

3. Google user data and Drive

Seedplanner requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata scope. This grants the app access only to a private, hidden application-data folder inside your own Google Drive — it does not give us access to any of your other Drive files. We use this folder solely to store and sync the data you enter into Seedplanner, so you can access your plan across devices.

Your data in this folder is controlled by you. You can delete it at any time, and you can revoke Seedplanner's access from your Google Account permissions page.

4. Limited Use disclosure

Seedplanner's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only use Google user data to provide and improve user-facing features of Seedplanner.
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide or improve the Service, to comply with applicable law, or as part of a merger or acquisition.
  • We do not use Google user data for serving advertisements.
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google user data unless we have your consent for specific messages, it is necessary for security purposes (such as investigating abuse), to comply with applicable law, or the data has been aggregated and anonymized.
  • We do not use Google user data to develop, improve, or train generalized or non-personalized artificial intelligence or machine-learning models.

5. How your data is stored and protected

Seedplanner is local-first. Your financial data lives in your browser's local storage on your device and, when signed in, in your personal Google Drive app-data folder. Data in transit to Google is protected by HTTPS/TLS. Seedplanner runs entirely in your browser; we operate no servers of our own and never receive a copy of your financial data. Our website is delivered as static files by a web-hosting provider that stores no personal data on our behalf.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only with service providers strictly necessary to operate the Service, when required by law, or to protect our rights and users. We do not use third-party processors to handle your financial data — it stays on your device and in your own Google Drive. Our website itself is served by a static web-hosting provider.

7. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you use the Service. You can remove it at any time by deleting entries in the app, clearing your browser storage, deleting the Seedplanner folder from your Google Drive, or revoking access via your Google Account. To request deletion of any data we hold, contact us at plannerstorebymaki@gmail.com.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at plannerstorebymaki@gmail.com.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at plannerstorebymaki@gmail.com, Seedplanner, Sydney, NSW, Australia.
